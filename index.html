About Us

CoolDeck Where innovation meets durability, delivering sustainable cooling solutions for a cooler planet.

COOLING SYSTEM PVT LTD was Established in the year 2014, Cooldeck Cooling System has made a well-recognized name as an manufacturer, wholesaler, retailer and service provider of PVC Fill, Cooling Tower, Chiller, V Bar,Cooling Tower Nozzle, PVC End Cap, Turn Buckle, Tower Sprinkler, Cooling Tower Chemicals and much more.

Cooldeck Cooling System is a Delhi-based cooling tower manufacturing company specializing in the manufacture and installation of standard and bespoke cooling towers for various industries. Our cooling towers meet and even exceed all industry standards and our post-installation services ensure that you get the maximum returns for your investment.

Our main focus is to build superior quality cooling towers using durable corrosion resistant materials designed to meet the demanding requirements of the cooling tower environment and local guidelines.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Cooling System Pvt Ltd ensure optimal performance and energy efficiency in their cooling products?

PVC fills enhance heat transfer by increasing the surface area for water-to-air contact in cooling towers.

Paper fills are an alternative to PVC fills, made from cellulose material, providing efficient water distribution and cooling.

An air washer humidifies and cleans air simultaneously, removing impurities and maintaining optimal humidity levels in indoor environments.

AHU is a crucial component in HVAC systems, responsible for conditioning and circulating air within a building, controlling temperature, humidity, and air quality.

Air washers find applications in various industries, such as manufacturing, data centers, and healthcare, where maintaining clean and humidified air is crucial.
