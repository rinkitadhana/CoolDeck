Cooling Towers are an essential component of many industrial processes, as they efficiently remove excess heat from water systems. These towers operate by using a combination of evaporation and air flow to cool the water, which is then recirculated back into the system. They are commonly used in power plants, chemical processing facilities, and other industrial settings where heat removal is critical.
