Cooling Towers

Cooling Towers are an essential component of many industrial processes, as they efficiently remove excess heat from water systems. These towers operate by using a combination of evaporation and air flow to cool the water, which is then recirculated back into the system. They are commonly used in power plants, chemical processing facilities, and other industrial settings where heat removal is critical. Our company specializes in providing high-quality cooling tower solutions that are tailored to meet the unique needs of each client. PVC Fills

Cooling tower fills put as much water surface area in contact with as much air as possible, for the longest amount of time possible. Film fills allow the water to form thin flowing sheets to expose as much water surface area as possible to the interacting flow.

Chiller Plants

Drift Eliminators

Drift Eliminators are used to capture water droplets in the air stream that otherwise would be lost to the atmosphere. Entrapping these droplets is important for not only conserving water as resource but also to prevent cooling tower water from contaminating the area peripheral to the Cooling Tower.

Cooldeck Cooling System Pvt. Ltd’s Drift Eliminators are specifically designed to achieve maximum drift removal efficiency in both cross flow and counter flow tower applications with various product options available to minimize pressure drop, drift loss, cost, or a combination of all three.
